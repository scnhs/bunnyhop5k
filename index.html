About

The Bunny Hop 5K is an annual race that raises funds and awareness for charity. The run is hosted by the State College Area High School National Honor Society.

All proceeds from the second annual Bunny Hop 5K will directly support the Four Diamonds in honor of Maryann Newcomb, a State High graduate and former NHS member who is bravely battling leukemia. The Four Diamonds works to conquer childhood cancer by covering the expenses of families and supporting innovative, life-saving research.

Four Diamonds

The Four Diamonds is a charitable organization based out of the Penn State Milton S. Hershey Medical Center. Its ultimate objective is to "conquer childhood cancer".

Additional Information

The Bunny Hop 5K will be held on Saturday, April 7th at the State College Area High School. The main event is centered in the soccer field (behind Welch Pool). Race start time will be at 9am.

Both runners and walkers are encouraged to participate, as well as teams. Costumes are allowed and encouraged for this fun event in support of childhood cancer patients. On race day, prizes will be given to the best-dressed team, fastest runners, the team which raises the most money, and more! Food and beverages will be provided.

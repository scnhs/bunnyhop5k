About

Bunny Hop 5K is an annual race that raises funds and awareness for charity. The run is hosted by the State College Area High School National Honor Society.

All proceeds from the first annual Bunny Hop 5K will directly support the Four Diamonds Fund in support of Maryann Newcomb, a former State High student who is bravely battling leukemia. The Four Diamonds Fund works to conquer childhood cancer by covering the expenses of families and supporting innovative, life-saving research.

Additional Information

The Bunny Hop 5K will be on Saturday, April 8th at Circleville Park. Race start time will be at 9am, with registration beginning at 8am.

Both runners and walkers are encouraged to participate, as well as teams. Costumes are allowed and encouraged for this fun event in support of childhood cancer patients. On race day, prizes will be given to the best-dressed team, fastest runners, the team which raises the most money, and more! Food and beverages will be provided.
